About Us

Our History

Archie's Diner was founded in 1957 by Archie Little, the famous North American Pinochle champion of the 1940s. His love of food and appetite for success drove him to open with his winnings one of Toronto's oldest diners still around today. Back then if you asked him for his famous burger recipe, he'd challenge you to a game of Pinochle for it.

Today, Archie's Diner continues to stand for good, fresh food and close-knit community. With the fourth generation of the family now managing the diner and the fourth generation of customers continuing to enjoy our home cooking, we take our central spot in Toronto seriously.

Come try our great menu which includes everything from mouthwatering home-made meatloaf to delectable spinach ricotta cannelloni.

Come by and become part of the family!

Location

Our diner is located at 15 College Street in Toronto just across from the university and just down the street from the historic Cabbagetown. Drop in for a bite to eat and a slice of pie.
